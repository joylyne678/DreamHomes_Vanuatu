About DreamHomes Vanuatu

DreamHomes Vanuatu is a locally owned real estate agency dedicated to helping clients purchase and rent properties across the islands. Our mission is to provide trustworthy, professional, and friendly service.

Our History

Founded in 2020, DreamHomes has grown to become one of Vanuatu’s most reliable real estate companies, offering a variety of beachfront homes, townhouses, and investment properties.

Our Services

We offer property sales, rentals, consulting, and property management services to both local and international clients.
